Monkiki App

Monkiki is an app that matches you with available pets in nearby shelters suited to your lifestyle and preferences.

Time: 4 to 5 Weeks

Tools: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Visual Studio, ReactJS, HTML, CSS.

Introduction

Challenge

Develop a landing page promoting the app.

Design the visual concept of IOS app.

Crafting the branding of the final product

My Role

I was in charge of the visual outcome of the product. From sketching to the final product. Developed the visual style guide for the brand and spec docs for future designers to stick to consistency and have a coherent brand and for devs to help them build the product.

Tools Used

User personas, competitive and comparative analysis, app flow, concept design, visual boards, UX Design, UI Design, Landing Page Development.

The Process

Research

To better understand the current market and users expectations, we did a competitive/ comparative analysis.

Comparative / Competitive Analysis

Pet Finder

Pet Finder helps user search for available dogs, cats, puppies and kittens to adopt. They are able to filter results by location, breed, age, size and gender.

Tinder

Dating apps that allow users to “like” or “dislike” potential matches. Mutual liked matches are saved and can be interacted with.

User Personas

Jane Doe

Jane wants to adopt another cat so that her current cat wouldn’t be lonely when she is at work.

She is very specific about what type of cat she wants so that she can be sure that the new pet suits her lifestyle and both pets would get along well.

John Doe

John loves animals and wants to adopt a pet, specifically a big dog. This will be his first time owning a pet.

John wants to see what dogs are available in nearby shelters for different preferences, to gauge which of them is a right fit for him.

Design

Setting up from the beginning the visual system helps us to provide a cohesive branding and coherent touchpoints.

Friendly & Simple

Our goal was to emulate the feeling of owning a pet. The warm palette relates to the soft cozy feeling of a pet’s embrace while the white shades add clarity to the design.
